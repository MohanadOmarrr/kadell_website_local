About Us

Kadell meaning gift of god, Kadell was created keeping in mind greatest gift god has given us. Kadell isn’t just another salon. It’s a safe heaven where women are welcome to embrace their beauty and flaws.

We are living in the world where everything is perfected, and the truth is perfect doesn’t exist. We in Kadell help you to reach the best version of you, we want women to leave our salon with a better version of their true selves, believing that beauty starts from within, so let’s free ourselves from all the stereotypes and learn to love ourselves truly and fully.
